About the Store

Reliable essentials for everyday living

Tindahan ni Aling Mila offers a wide variety of basic essentials for daily household and small business needs. Customers can shop for rice grain, hotdogs, canned goods, plastic cups, seasoning, herbs, spices, and many other practical items at affordable prices.

Our store is conveniently located for easy access and is open 24/7, making it easier for customers to buy what they need at any time of the day. We offer both retail and wholesale options, so you can purchase the quantity that fits your personal or business needs.

We currently accept cash payments, keeping every transaction simple, direct, and hassle-free.
